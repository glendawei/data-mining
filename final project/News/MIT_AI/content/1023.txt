Robot reflections 
 The MIT Museum's latest exhibit, Robots and Beyond, explores the world of artificial intelligence, including robots from MIT's AI Lab. Hanging in the case is Hannibal, a small, six-legged robot. Below it is Pebbles, a visually guided obstacle avoidance system built to gather geological samples for the Mars Rover Project.  
A version of this article appeared in MIT Tech Talk on November 8, 2000.
