Ford Executive Chair Bill Ford and German Chancellor Olaf Scholz to Open New Ford Cologne Electric Vehicle Centre Ford executive chair Bill Ford and German Chancellor Olaf Scholz will officially open the Ford Cologne Electric Vehicle Centre on Monday, June 12. Ford has transformed its historic Cologne plant as part of a $2 billion investment to build a new generation of electric passenger vehicles for millions of European customers. The event will also be attended by Ford Model e general manager Europe Martin Sander, Ford employees and other VIP guests. A live stream of the opening will begin at 1330 UK time on Monday at https://youtube.com/live/ywM3T4rkcyc SOURCE: Ford
