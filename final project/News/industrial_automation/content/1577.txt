OTTO Motors Launches Midsize Autonomous Mobile Robot OTTO Motors, a provider of autonomous mobile robots (AMRs), unveiled its newest AMR, OTTO 600. The AMR unlocks new workflows with its ability to move pallets, carts and other payloads up to 600 kg (1,322 lb).
