OTTO Motors launches midsize AMR; releases new software OTTO Motors introduced its newest AMR – OTTO 600. The new product is the company’s toughest and most nimble AMR in its class, according to an OTTO press statement. OTTO 600 unlocks new workflows with its ability to move pallets, carts and other payloads up to 600 kg (1,322 lb). It features an all-metal construction and IP54 rating. OTTO 600 is available through the existing global network of OTTO Motors system integrators and material handling solution providers. OTTO’s latest software release, 2.28 features options to deliver faster and safer traffic movement and a new facility configuration interface for quicker workflow set-up, according to the company. This is the sixth software release from the company in the last three years.
