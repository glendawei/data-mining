2023 PTDA Canadian conference to take place in June The Power Transmission Distributors Association’s (PTDA) Canadian conference is set to take place from June 6 to 8 in Ottawa, Ont. Registration for the event will open in late February. The event offers networking and knowledge-building for power transmission and motion control Canadian distributors and manufacturers doing business in Canada. Throughout the conference attendees engage in learning and conversation to broaden their network, expand connections and deepen business relationships. The conference offers distributors one-on-one access to key decision makers at U.S. and Canadian manufacturers to diversify their supplier base during DM-IDEX.
