ATS Corporation to host 2023 Institutional investor Day in New York ATS Corporation, formerly known as ATS Automation Tooling Systems, is hosting an in-person Institutional Investor Day on Thursday, February 23, 2023. The company will hold the Institutional Investor Day at the InterContinental New York Times Square in New York, NY. Its corporate presentation is scheduled for 8:30 a.m. ET.  The presentation will feature the ATS executive team providing an update on corporate strategy, an overview of key business units and a review of ATS’ financial position. The investor day will be limited to institutional investors and analysts only. ATS requests registrants for the in-person event to use the following registration link.
