PTDA’s workforce initiative adds resources to support industry employers The Power Transmission Distributors Association’s charitable arm, the PTDA Foundation, has added resources to help the power transmission and motion control industry employers in their recruitment and retention efforts. Through the PT WORK Force initiative, the association is offering programs and services to assist in this area. All the resources can be accessed, free of charge, at PTWORKForce.org.
