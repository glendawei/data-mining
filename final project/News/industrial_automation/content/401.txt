Wago promotes Jesse Cox to automation leadership position Wago announced the promotion of Jesse Cox to the position of director of automation sales engineering and development. Cox has been in the company for around 10 years now. Most recently, he was the senior sales and application engineer for Linux and IIOT. As the director of automation sales engineering and development, Cox will continue to serve the company’s sales efforts. Wago is currently focused on a highly technical, solutions-based approach to automation that centers around people and process. “I want to continue the upward trajectory of growth and innovation to expand the most capable automation team in the market,” says Cox.
