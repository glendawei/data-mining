Pilz Canada supports upcoming seminar on machine safety Pilz Canada announced that it is sponsoring the Functional Safety and Validation Seminar that is set to take place from May 26 to 27. The seminar will provide an overview of international standards for machinery safety and their application. These standards provide manufacturers of machines with the basic technical rules for the design of safe machinery. The seminar is intended to transmit to the participants the basic knowledge of these standards and their use in the industrial area. The seminar will also show practical applications as examples. The speakers will offer information as experts involved in the standardization of machinery safety. Seminar registration details are available here.
