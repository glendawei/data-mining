Registrations open for PTDA Canadian Conference The Power Transmission Distributors Association is hosting its Canadian Conference from June 7 to 9 at the DoubleTree by Hilton in Montreal, Que. Registrations are currently open. This event for power transmission and motion control Canadian distributors and manufacturers doing business in Canada is back after a three-year hiatus. Attendees will have the opportunity to network, connect and build relationships with their industry peers. The conference is offering: All attendees will receive the latest news on industry trends and happenings. They will have the opportunity to enhance their industry knowledge of top trends, insights and data. They can also converse with thought leadership with proven expertise who are considered reliable sources of information, opinions and insights. Further details are available here.
