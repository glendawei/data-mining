Lafert North America appoints new account manager for the region Lafert North America has announced the appointment of a new key account manager for its North America team – Ron Csermak. Csermak brings with him 30 years of experience in the electrical and electronics industry to his new role. He has previously held roles in engineering design, product management, sales and business development and key account management. Most recently, Csermak was working as an account manager selling motors and AC drives. He has also worked as a business development manager previously and has won awards in that capacity as the top salesperson selling AC drives, automation and motor control solutions. Csermak holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering and Management from McMaster University and is a licensed Professional Engineer in Ontario.
