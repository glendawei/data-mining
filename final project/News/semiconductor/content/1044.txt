EPC launches 1kW 48V/12V  LLC converter in 17.5mm x 22.8mm footprint Efficient  Power Conversion Corp (EPC) of El Segundo, CA, USA – which makes  enhancement-mode gallium nitride on silicon (eGaN) power field-effect  transistors (FETs) and integrated circuits for power management  applications – has announced  the availability of the EPC9159, a 48V/12V LLC converter designed for high-density 48V  server power and DC-DC converters.
