Aixtron launches G10-GaN MOCVD platform for power and RF devices At the  SEMICON Taiwan 2023 event in Taipei (6-8 September), deposition equipment maker  Aixtron SE of Herzogenrath, near Aachen, Germany has launched its new G10-GaN cluster metal-organic chemical vapor deposition  (MOCVD) platform for gallium nitride  (GaN)-based  power and radio frequency (RF)  devices, offering what is claimed to be best-in-class performance, an all-new  compact design, and overall lowest cost per wafer.
