Spalling-induced GaN lift-off and transfer Air Force Research Laboratory, KBR Inc and Agnitron  Technology in the USA have applied spalling-induced van der Waals  (vdW) epitaxial lift-off (ELO) and transfer methods to 4-inch gallium nitride (GaN) films [Michael  Snure et al, Journal of Applied Physics, v134, p025307, 2023].
