Navitas and Plexim accelerate time-to-market with PLECS models for GeneSiC power semiconductors Gallium nitride  (GaN) power IC and silicon carbide  (SiC) technology firm Navitas Semiconductor of Torrance, CA, USA and  Plexim GmbH of Zurich, Switzerland, which provides design and testing  tools for the power conversion industry, have partnered to release GeneSiC G3 SiC MOSFET  and Gen 5 MPS diode PLECS thermal loss models for highly  accurate simulations of complete power electronics systems.
