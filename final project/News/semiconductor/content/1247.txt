Infineon makes available new-generation 1200V CoolSiC trench MOSFET in TO263-7 package Infineon Technologies AG of  Munich, Germany has made available its new generation of 1200V CoolSiC  MOSFETs in TO263-7 packaging for automotive applications. The automotive-graded  silicon carbide (SiC) MOSFET generation offers high power density and  efficiency, enables bi-directional charging and significantly reduces system cost  in on-board charging (OBC) and DC–DC applications.
