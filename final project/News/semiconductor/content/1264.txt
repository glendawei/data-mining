MACOM giving live demonstrations of new products at IMS MACOM Technology Solutions Inc of Lowell,  MA, USA (which designs and makes RF, microwave, analog and mixed-signal and  optical semiconductor technologies) is hosting live demonstrations of a wide range of new products in  booth #1135 and  foundry services in booth #722 at  the IEEE’s International Microwave Symposium (IMS 2023) in San Diego,  CA, USA (13–15 June). The RF & Microwave IC and Module design, product management  and applications engineering teams are providing in-depth explanations of  MACOM’s solutions and benefits. Customers can ask questions and interact with  MACOM staff during the demonstrations, which feature new product additions to  the firm’s portfolio of RF and microwave IC and module solutions.
