CGD launches second series of ICeGaN 650V HEMTs Fabless semiconductor company Cambridge GaN Devices Ltd  (CGD) — which was spun out of the University of Cambridge Department of  Engineering’s Electrical Power and Energy Conversion group in 2016  and designs, develops and commercializes power transistors and ICs that  use GaN-on-silicon substrates — has launched the second series of its ICeGaN 650V gallium nitride high-electron-mobility transistor  (HEMT) family.
