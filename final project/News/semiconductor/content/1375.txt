Riber receives order from Asia for MBE 6000 production system Riber S.A. of Bezons, France – which manufactures  molecular beam epitaxy (MBE) systems as well as evaporation sources –says that a new industrial  customer in Asia has placed an order worth  several million euros for a MBE 6000  production system, for delivery in 2024, giving it production  capacity for advanced electronic components for both telecoms and datacoms  applications. Riber says that the new  firm order is in line with the current dynamics of industrial MBE markets. With about 40  machines in operation worldwide, the MBE 6000 is claimed to be the benchmark  MBE system for the mass production of electronic and  optoelectronic components used in telecoms and in fiber-optic networks.
