Transphorm and Weltrend partner to release integrated GaN system-in-package Transphorm Inc of Goleta, near Santa Barbara,  CA, USA — which designs and manufactures JEDEC- and AEC-Q101-qualified gallium nitride  (GaN) field-effect transistors (FETs) for high-voltage power conversion — and fabless firm Weltrend  Semiconductor Inc of Hsinchu Science Park, Taiwan,  which specializes  in the design, testing, application development and distribution of  mixed-signal/digital ICs in power supplies, motor controls and image processing, have  released their first GaN system-in-package (SiP). The new  WT7162RHUG24A SiP integrates Weltrend’s WT7162RHSG08 multi-mode flyback PWM controller with  Transphorm’s 240mΩ, 650V SuperGaN  FET.
