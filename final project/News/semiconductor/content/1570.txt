Emcore announces pricing of public offering Emcore Corp of Alhambra, CA, USA – which  provides mixed-signal products for the aerospace & defense, communications  and sensing markets – has  announced the pricing of a public offering of 15,454,546 shares of its common stock  at a price of $1.10 per share. Gross  proceeds to Emcore, before deducting the  placement agent fees and other offering expenses, are expected to be about  $17m. The offering is expected to close on or about 17 February, subject to customary closing conditions.  A.G.P./Alliance Global Partners is acting as sole placement agent on a  best-efforts basis. Emcore leverages photonic integrated circuit  (PIC), quartz MEMS, lithium niobate,  and indium phosphide chip-level  technology to deliver component and system-level products across end-market  applications including navigation and inertial sensing, defense  optoelectronics, broadband communications, optical sensing, and specialty chips  for telecom and data centers. The  firm has vertically integrated manufacturing capability at its wafer  fabrication facilities in Alhambra, CA, Budd Lake,  NJ, Concord, CA and Tinley Park, IL.  Emcore’s manufacturing facilities maintain ISO 9001  quality management certification,  and the firm is AS9100 aerospace quality certified at its facilities in  Budd Lake and Concord.
