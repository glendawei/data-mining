VisIC demos efficient GaN-based 3-phase traction inverter with automotive-grade PMSM motor VisIC Technologies Ltd of Ness Ziona, Israel – a fabless supplier of  power conversion devices based on gallium nitride (GaN) transistors  for high-voltage automotive applications – says that it has successfully  tested its 2.2mΩ 650V half-bridge power module, consisting of four parallel  8mΩ power FET, in a 3-phase configuration on a dyno-test-bench using a permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) motor at a  major automotive OEM.
