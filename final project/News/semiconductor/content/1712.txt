EPC shipping lowest on-resistance 150V and 200V GaN FETs on market Efficient  Power Conversion Corp (EPC) of El Segundo, CA, USA – which makes  enhancement-mode gallium nitride on silicon (eGaN) power field-effect  transistors (FETs) and integrated circuits for power management  applications – has launched the 150V, 3mΩ EPC2305 and the 200V, 5mΩ EPC2304  GaN FETs in a thermally  enhanced QFN package with exposed top and a small 3mm x 5mm footprint.
