EPC launches 80V, 4mΩ GaN FET in 1.5mm x 2.5mm footprint Efficient  Power Conversion Corp (EPC) of El Segundo, CA, USA – which makes  enhancement-mode gallium nitride on silicon (eGaN) power field-effect  transistors (FETs) and integrated circuits for power management applications –  has launched  the 80V, 4mΩ EPC2619  as the lead product of a new generation of eGaN devices that have double the  power density compared with EPC’s  prior-generation products.
