First Solar completes sale of 665MW Japan O&M platform to PAG Cadmium telluride (CdTe) thin-film photovoltaic  (PV) module maker First Solar Inc of Tempe, AZ, USA has completed the  sale of its solar operations and maintenance (O&M) platform in Japan (announced  in May) to funds managed by Tokyo-based PAG Real Assets (PAG), an Asia Pacific-focused private investment  manager with more than $50bn in assets under its management.
