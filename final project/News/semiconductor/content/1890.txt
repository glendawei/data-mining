KLA to build new R&D and manufacturing facility for SPTS in Newport, Wales Process control and inspection systems provider  KLA Corp of Milpitas, CA, USA plans to build a new  R&D and manufacturing center for its SPTS division in Newport, Wales, UK (which manufactures etch, PVD and CVD wafer processing solutions for the MEMS, advanced packaging,  LED, high-speed RF, and power management  device markets).
