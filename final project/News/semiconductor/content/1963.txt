Toshiba launches third-generation SiC MOSFETs Tokyo-based Toshiba Electronic Devices & Storage Corp  (TDSC) - spun off from Toshiba Corp in 2017 - has launched the new TWxxNxxxC series of power devices, its third-generation silicon carbide (SiC) metal-oxide-semiconductor  field-effect transistors (MOSFETs), delivering low on-resistance and  significantly reduced switching loss.
