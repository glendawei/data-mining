Riber receives order from  Asia for additional MBE 6000 production system Riber S.A.  of Bezons, France – which manufactures molecular beam epitaxy (MBE) systems as well as evaporation  sources and effusion cells – has received an order worth several million euros for an MBE 6000 production system, for delivery in 2023. An industrial customer in Asia that already owns several MBE 6000 systems is  boosting its production capacity for electronic devices. With nearly 40 machines in operation worldwide, the  MBE 6000 is  claimed to be the benchmark MBE system for the mass production of  electronic and optoelectronic device material used in telecommunications and in  fiber-optic networks.
