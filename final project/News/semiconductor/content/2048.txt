First Solar issues 2022 Sustainability Report showcasing progress on ESG metrics Cadmium telluride (CdTe) thin-film photovoltaic  (PV) module maker First Solar Inc of Tempe, AZ, USA has issued its 2022 Sustainability Report  highlighting its continued environmental, social and governance (ESG) progress.
