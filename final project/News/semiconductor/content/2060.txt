Aixtron to ship CCS MOCVD system to University of Texas at Austin for gallium oxide power devices Deposition equipment maker  Aixtron SE of Herzogenrath, near Aachen, Germany is to ship a  new Close Coupled  Showerhead (CCS) metal-organic chemical vapor deposition (MOCVD) system  to The University of  Texas at Austin, Microelectronics Research Center (MRC), Department of Electrical  and Computer Engineering.
