Obducat receives SEK7.1m order for fully automated Microcluster Track wet processing tool Obducat AB of Lund, Sweden says that its  subsidiary Obducat Europe GmbH, a supplier of systems for lithography  processing, has received an order worth about SEK7.1m for a fully automated  Microcluster Track wet processing tool. To be delivered in first-quarter 2023, the system will be used for  cleaning processes for silicon carbide  (SiC) substrates.
