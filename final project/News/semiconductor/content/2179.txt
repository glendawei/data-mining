Qorvo launches 20W GaN-on-SiC MMIC power amplifier for satcoms Qorvo Inc of Greensboro, NC, USA (which provides  core technologies and RF solutions for mobile, infrastructure and defense  applications) has launched a  20W GaN-on-SiC (gallium nitride on silicon nitride)  power amplifier (PA) for defense and  commercial satellite applications, including low Earth orbit (LEO) constellations.
