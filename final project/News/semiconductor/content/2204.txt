EPC launches smallest 40V, 1.1mΩ FET Efficient Power  Conversion Corp (EPC) of El Segundo, CA, USA – which makes  enhancement-mode gallium nitride on silicon (eGaN) power  field-effect transistors (FETs) and integrated circuits for power management  applications – has expanded  its selection of low-voltage, off-the-shelf gallium nitride transistors with the introduction of the EPC2066 (0.8mΩ typical, 40V) GaN FET.
