GaN Systems and Phihong debut highest-power-density gaming laptop power supply GaN Systems Inc of Ottawa, Ontario, Canada (a fabless developer of  gallium nitride-based power switching semiconductors for power  conversion and control applications) and power supply company Phihong Technology have announced  the debut of Phihong’s 280W GaN charger (claimed to be the industry’s  highest-power-density power supply for gaming laptops). The new  GaN charger is being showcased in GaN Systems’ booth #509  (Hall 9) at Power, Control and Intelligent Motion (PCIM) Europe 2022 in Nürnberg  Messe, Nuremberg, Germany (10–12 May).
