MACOM awarded US DoD development contract MACOM Technology Solutions Inc of Lowell,  MA, USA (which designs and makes semiconductors, components and subassemblies for  analog RF, microwave, millimeter-wave and photonic applications) has been awarded a contract from the US  Department of Defense (DoD) to develop a high-power transmitter. The contract requires MACOM to develop a 45kW radio frequency (RF) transmitter using gallium nitride (GaN) and antenna beam-forming  technology. The solution is based on the firm’s proprietary MACOM PURE CARBIDE  GaN components and power combining  expertise. “This award further validates that our semiconductor technology  and engineering expertise is suitable for very high-power DoD programs,” says  president & CEO Stephen G. Daly. “We look forward to supporting this and  other similar programs with our technology.”
