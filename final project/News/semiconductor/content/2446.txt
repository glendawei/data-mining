Gallium oxide film grown on 6-inch wafers  by HVPE A joint effort between Tokyo-based Taiyo  Nippon Sanso Corp (TNSC), Tokyo University of Agriculture and  Technology, Novel Crystal Technology Inc of Sayama, Saitama Prefecture,  Japan has succeeded in growing gallium oxide film on a 6-inch wafer by  halide vapor-phase epitaxy (HVPE).
