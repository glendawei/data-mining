IQE confirms 2021 revenue guidance of £164m, down 8% year-on-year In a pre-close trading update for full-year 2021,  epiwafer and  substrate maker IQE plc of Cardiff, UK says that it expects revenue to be in line  with its November trading update guidance of about £164m (down 8% year-on-year, at constant  currency), subject to external audit review. Cash capex spend for full-year 2021 is expected to be in line with previously  issued guidance of £14-17m. This should result in a net debt position at  end-2021 of about £6m. IQE will report its full year financial results on 29 March.
