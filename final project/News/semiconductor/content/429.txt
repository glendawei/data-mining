YMTC Working to Make Advanced Chips With Local Tech – SCMP The “top secret project” is aimed at realising Beijing’s ambitions of becoming self-reliant in critical semiconductor technology The “top secret project” is aimed at realising Beijing’s ambitions of becoming self-reliant in critical semiconductor technology  China’s top chipmaker Yangtze Memory Technologies Co (YMTC) is making progress in making advanced 3D NAND chips using locally sourced technology, the South China Morning Post reported. The “top secret project” is aimed at overcoming sanctions imposed by Washington and realising Beijing’s ambitions of becoming self-reliant in critical semiconductor technology, amid a chip war with the US.
