China Rushes to Hire Foreign Microchip Engineers – Nikkei China’s microchip firms are rushing to snap up foreign engineers in China who were laid off after a crackdown by the US China’s microchip firms are rushing to snap up foreign engineers in China who were laid off after a crackdown by the US  China’s microchip firms are rushing to snap up foreign engineers in China who were laid off as a result of US curbs, Nikkei Asia reported. The likes of California-based chip developer Marvell were forced to lay off hundreds of foreign workers in China, said the report, prompting local chipmakers to offer lucrative wages to take them on instead.
