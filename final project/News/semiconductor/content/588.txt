TSMC Plans Expansion With New 1nm Chip Fab – Taiwan News The proposed plant will be located in Toayuan in Northern Taiwan, with TSMC yet to confirm the claim which came from a close source, Taiwan News said The proposed plant will be located in Toayuan in Northern Taiwan, with TSMC yet to confirm the claim which came from a close source, Taiwan News said  Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) is planning to build a new factory to produce 1 nanometre chips, Taiwan News reported. The proposed plant will reportedly be located in Toayuan in Northern Taiwan, though the world’s top chipmaker has not yet confirmed the claim, which came from a close source.
