Huawei to Relaunch 5G Phone Despite US Sanctions – FT Huawei will likely avoid using sanctioned advanced chips and use less advanced alternatives from Chinese companies, the FT said Huawei will likely avoid using sanctioned advanced chips and use less advanced alternatives from Chinese companies, the FT said  China’s Huawei plans to relaunch its 5G phone as soon as next year despite the setback of US sanctions, The Financial Times reported.
