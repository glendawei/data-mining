Apple Playing With Fire Using Chinese Chips, Rubio Says – FT “We cannot allow Chinese companies beholden to the Communist Party into our telecommunications networks and millions of Americans’ iPhones,” Senator Marco Rubio said. “We cannot allow Chinese companies beholden to the Communist Party into our telecommunications networks and millions of Americans’ iPhones,” Senator Marco Rubio said.  A US senator said that Apple is “playing with fire” with its plans to use Chinese semiconductors for its iPhone 14, the Financial Times reported. Apple knows the security risks posed by China’s Yangtze Memory Technologies Co, Republican vice-chair of the Senate intelligence committee Marco Rubio said in the report. “We cannot allow Chinese companies beholden to the Communist Party into our telecommunications networks and millions of Americans’ iPhones,” he was quoted as saying.
