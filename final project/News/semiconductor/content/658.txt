The UK government has blocked a Hong Kong firm from acquiring a British semiconductor parts supplier due to national security concerns, according to a report by The Guardian. The decision to stop Super Orange HK from buying out the Bristol-based Pulsic is Britain’s latest move to protect its technological edge from Chinese ownership, the report said.
