Coolant Plant Closure Risks Chip Supply – BusinessKorea The plant in Zwijndrecht, which closed due to tightened environmental regulations, accounts for 80% of global total semiconductor coolant output The plant in Zwijndrecht, which closed due to tightened environmental regulations, accounts for 80% of global total semiconductor coolant output  The indefinite closure of 3M’s semiconductor coolant plant in Belgium could have major effects on the global semiconductor industry, BusinessKorea magazine reported. The 3M plant in Zwijndrecht closed on March 8 due to tightened environmental regulations. The plant accounts for 80% of the global total semiconductor coolant output.
