TSMC Could Increase Capex Spending – Commercial Times Its three-year investment plan of $100 billion may also be raised to $112 billion as it expands capacity in Taiwan, Japan, China and the US Its three-year investment plan of $100 billion may also be raised to $112 billion as it expands capacity in Taiwan, Japan, China and the US  Foundry leader Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company’s capital spending in 2022 could rise 40% to US$42 billion, Taipei’s Commercial Times reported. The previously announced three-year investment plan of $100 billion may also be raised to $112 billion as it expands capacity in Taiwan, Japan, China and the US.
