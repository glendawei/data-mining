imec and SMART Photonics sign MoU to work on hybrid integration of InP on SiN/SiPh Nanoelectronics research center imec of Leuven, Belgium and independent pure-play  indium phosphide (InP) photonic integrated circuit (PIC) foundry  SMART Photonics of Eindhoven, The Netherlands have signed a memorandum of understanding  (MOU) to underpin their intention to work together in the field of hybrid  integration of InP on silicon nitride/silicon photonics (SiN/SiPh).
