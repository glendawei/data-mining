Infineon completes acquisition of GaN Systems Infineon Technologies AG of Munich, Germany has closed its acquisition (announced on 2 March) of GaN Systems Inc of Ottawa, Ontario, Canada  (a fabless developer of gallium nitride-based power switching  semiconductors for power conversion and control applications) for US$830m,  funded from existing liquidity in an all-cash transaction. All required  regulatory clearances have been obtained and GaN Systems  is now part of Infineon.
