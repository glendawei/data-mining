Element Six selected for US Department of Defense LADDIS program CVD-based synthetic diamond materials firm  Element Six of Oxford, UK (E6, part of the De Beers Group)  has been selected as a performer for the LADDIS (Large Area Device-quality  Diamond Substrates) program, set up by the United States Defense Advanced Research  Projects Agency (DARPA). LADDIS  aims to develop new ways of fabricating device-quality diamond substrates with  ultimate applications that include radio-frequency and power electronics  to operate in the harsh environments of military applications.
